## About FuelPay

FuelPay is a premium Flutter fintech app that enables:

✨ **Fuel Payments** - Seamless UPI-backed payment flows for fuel purchases  
🎯 **Reward Tracking** - Earn and unlock tier-based rewards from Bronze to Diamond  
💳 **Wallet Management** - Track fuel spend and transaction history  
📍 **Station Validation** - QR codes and geofence-based transaction verification  
🛡️ **Fraud Prevention** - Secure payment flows with merchant validation

## Key Highlights

## Core Tech Stack

- **Framework**: Flutter 3.x with Dart 3.9+
- **State Management**: Riverpod
- **Routing**: GoRouter
- **Networking**: Dio
- **Storage**: Hive & Flutter Secure Storage
- **Payments**: Razorpay Flutter
- **Location**: Geolocator & Google Maps
- **Backend**: Firebase
