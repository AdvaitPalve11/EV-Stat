# FuelPay Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FuelPay handles information when the mobile app, web app, or related services are used.

## Information We May Collect

- Account and contact information such as phone number or email address
- Transaction and payment metadata
- Device information and app diagnostics
- Location data when required for station discovery, geofencing, or fraud prevention
- Usage data such as app interactions, performance logs, and feature usage

## How We Use Information

We may use information to:

- Provide payments, rewards, and wallet features
- Verify station-based transactions
- Improve app performance and reliability
- Detect fraud, abuse, and policy violations
- Send notifications and service messages

## How Information Is Shared

FuelPay does not sell personal information. Information may be shared with:

- Payment and financial service providers
- Firebase or push-notification infrastructure
- Maps and location service providers
- Hosting, analytics, and security vendors used to operate the service

We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect users, the app, or the service.

## Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

## Security

FuelPay uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No system is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Location Data

Location data may be used for nearby station discovery, geofence validation, and anti-fraud checks. Users can manage location permissions in their device settings, but some features may not work without location access.

## Children’s Privacy

FuelPay is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Choices

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information. Requests can be sent using the contact method below.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above.

## Contact

For privacy requests or questions, open an issue in the repository or contact the maintainers through the official project channels.
